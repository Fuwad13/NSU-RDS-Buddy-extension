NSU RDS Buddy

Version: 0.1.0

Description: A Chrome extension to calculate what-if grades and cgpa for NSUers

Author: Fuwad Hasan

Source code: Github

This extension provides enhanced functionalities for NSU RDS including:

  • CGPA What-If Calculator
  • Visualization of grades & cgpa over time

How to use:

Please go to your grade history page in RDS and scroll to the bottom to see the extension's functionalities
